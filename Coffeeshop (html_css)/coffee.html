Espresso coffee

  1. Wipe the portafilter with a clean, dry cloth.
  2. Dose the coffee grounds into the portafilter.
  3. Level the dose and tamp it evenly.
  4. Flush the group head.
  5. Attach the portafilter and immediately start the brewing.
  6. Flush the group head. Place the cups below the portafilter spouts. Enjoy!
